Garage Hub

Co-founder of a fablab in Kharkiv. From a rented garage to a self-sustainable open workshop. 5 years of fun!

This page redirects to facebook page or to the official site garagehub.org. Also, check out the instagram page

In August 2014 together with friends we rented a garage to play around with engineering and electronics as a hobby. We took part in physics and engineering competitions, designed art installations, participated in hackatons. In February 2017 we opened a complete fabrication laboratory with CNC machines and 3d printers accessible for the members of our open community, using grant funding.
